# Christmas Sales Analysis (SQL)

## Overview
This project analyzes sales performance across multiple Christmas seasons to identify revenue trends, business risks, and growth patterns.

## Dataset
- Multi-year retail sales data
- Includes revenue, quantity sold, cost, and profit
- Filtered to include only complete Christmas seasons

## Skills Used
- SQL (CTEs, aggregations, growth rate calculations)
- Data cleaning and filtering
- Trend analysis

## Key Findings

### Revenue Trends
- Revenue declined in 2020–2021 (-3.2%)
- Recovery in 2021–2022 was weak

### Growth Drivers
- Growth was driven primarily by quantity (volume), not pricing
- No strong channel or product significantly boosted performance

### Business Insights
- Revenue volatility increases business risk
- Weak recovery suggests slow business momentum
- Lack of strong growth drivers limits scalability

## Recommendations
- Identify and scale high-performing channels or products
- Improve pricing strategies to increase margins
- Strengthen demand through targeted campaigns
